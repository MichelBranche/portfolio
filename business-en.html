FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

How long does it take?

It depends on scope and content. One page sites are faster, larger sites take a bit more iteration.

Do I need a logo and final texts?

No. If you have materials, great. Otherwise I can help you structure and polish texts and images.

Where will the website be hosted?

We can use GitHub Pages for simple builds, or pick a dedicated domain and hosting depending on your needs.

Can I update the website later?

Yes. We can set things up to make updates easy, or handle them together.
